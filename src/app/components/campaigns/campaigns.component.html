What We Did?

Our Campaigns

Since our founding on March 5, 2024, Wings of Kindness has been dedicated to transforming lives across Islamabad with the belief that your small donations can give big smiles! Through our diverse campaigns, we’ve reached out to the needy, orphans, widows, and our environment, creating a ripple effect of compassion and change. Below, explore the heart of our efforts and the impact we’ve made together.

Ration Drive

Our Ration Drive is a cornerstone of our mission to support the poor, needy, and widows in Islamabad. We carefully curated ration bags containing 16 essential items to provide sustenance and hope. Over the past year, we’ve conducted multiple Ration Drives, reaching countless families and ensuring no one goes hungry. Each bag is a symbol of care, made possible by our generous donors and dedicated volunteers.

Anaaj Drive

The Anaaj Drive focuses on providing vital food staples like wheat, pulses, and other essentials to the needy and widows. By addressing basic nutritional needs, we empower families to thrive. This campaign reflects our commitment to tackling hunger at its roots, delivering not just food but also dignity and support to those who need it most.

Orphanage Visits

Our visits to orphanages across Islamabad bring joy and warmth to the lives of orphans. We donate thoughtfully prepared goodie bags, filled with treats and essentials, to children in various orphanages. These visits are more than just donations—they’re moments of connection, laughter, and love, creating lasting memories for the children we serve.

Tree Plantation Drive

In our Tree Plantation Drive, we worked to make Islamabad greener by planting shade trees throughout the city. These trees, chosen for their ability to provide shelter and coolness, contribute to a healthier environment for all. Our volunteers came together to plant hope for future generations, fostering a sustainable and vibrant community.

Cleanliness Drive

Our Cleanliness Drive mobilized volunteers to clean public spaces in Islamabad, promoting a cleaner and healthier city. By picking up litter and beautifying our surroundings, we inspired others to take pride in their environment. This campaign reflects our belief that a clean community is a thriving community.

Water Pots for Birds

With compassion for all living beings, we installed water pots at various points across Islamabad to provide hydration for birds. This small yet meaningful initiative ensures that our feathered friends have access to clean water, especially during harsh weather, showcasing our commitment to caring for every part of our ecosystem.

Educational and Advocacy Initiatives

Wings of Kindness is committed to empowering students by connecting them with opportunities to engage in meaningful dialogues and experiences. We organized visits to the National Assembly, offering students insights into governance and civic responsibility. Our participation in the Women Film Festival celebrated women’s stories and creativity. We facilitated a panel discussion on inclusive rights for people with disabilities, amplifying voices for accessibility and equality. IT students explored cutting-edge innovations through a visit to the Artificial Intelligence Business Conference. We also arranged participation in a panel discussion on climate change and ecosystem preservation hosted by the Swiss & Canadian Embassy, and a panel discussion on human rights and women’s rights by Alliance Française. These initiatives, along with many others tailored for students in relevant fields, inspire and equip the next generation to drive positive change.

Looking Ahead

Wings of Kindness is always planning new ways to serve our community. With several exciting campaigns in the pipeline, we’re committed to expanding our impact and continuing our mission of compassion. Stay tuned for updates, and join us in making a difference—because every small act of kindness can create big smiles!
